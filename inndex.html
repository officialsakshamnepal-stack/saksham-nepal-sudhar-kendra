About Us / हाम्रो बारेमा

Saksham Nepal Sudhar Kendra is a rehabilitation center dedicated to helping individuals overcome drug and alcohol addiction. Our mission is to guide people toward a healthy, meaningful, and drug-free life through care, discipline, and compassion.

सक्षम नेपाल सुधार केन्द्र नशा तथा मदिरा दुर्व्यसनबाट पीडित व्यक्तिहरूको पुनःस्थापनाका लागि समर्पित एक विश्वासयोग्य पुनर्वास केन्द्र हो। हामी अनुशासन, करुणा, आत्मसम्मान र निरन्तर सहयोगमार्फत व्यक्तिहरूलाई स्वस्थ, सम्मानजनक र नशामुक्त जीवनतर्फ अघि बढ्न सहयोग गर्छौं।

Our Recovery Program

Our program focuses on physical, mental, emotional, and spiritual healing. We follow a structured daily routine that includes counseling, group meetings, meditation, discipline, and personal responsibility.

"Recovery is not a destination, it is a journey – one day at a time."

Admission Process

  1. Initial phone inquiry or visit
  2. Assessment of addiction and health condition
  3. Family consultation and consent
  4. Admission and orientation
  5. Start of structured recovery program

Rules & Discipline

  • Zero tolerance for drugs and alcohol
  • Respect staff, fellow residents, and property
  • Participation in daily programs is mandatory
  • No violence, abuse, or illegal activities
  • Honesty, responsibility, and discipline are essential

NA – Twelve Traditions

Our recovery philosophy follows the Twelve Traditions of Narcotics Anonymous, emphasizing unity, service, anonymity, and carrying the message to addicts who still suffer.
